Meet The Owner

Over the last decade, Manan has planned and executed hundreds of events ranging from large-scale conventions to themed holiday parties to intimate networking dinners and everything in between. His creativity, attention to detail, organizational skills and passion for planning ensures the success of every celebration he’s a part of. Manan began her career working at the Hayatt Hotel, the largest hotel in Nepal, where he realized his love for planning, logistics, and all the little details that make a spectacular event. From there, he was recruited by the Director of Employee Programs at Partners HealthCare, where he planned corporate and employee recognition events for almost ten years. In 2013, Manan became the owner of Destiny Events and given his extensive background in corporate events, he shifted the company’s focus to corporate events. Manan is a proud Certified Meeting Professional (CMP), a credential and distinction of excellence in today’s meetings, conventions and exhibitions industry.
